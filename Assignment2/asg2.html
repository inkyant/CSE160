Add a Joint!
You can add your own shape to the scene, connect it to a parent, and specify around which axis it rotates and be able to rotate it! It will also play in the animation. "Rotation Center" is the point about which the rotation will happen, i.e. all zeros means spinning in place, so change this if you want it to spin about an end.
